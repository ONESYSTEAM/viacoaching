What We Do

Leadership Coaching with Operational Precision

We combine deep expertise in healthcare operations with the transformative power of executive coaching. Our approach is structured yet flexible, bold yet supportive - designed to meet professionals where they are and guide them toward where they want to be.

Our Services Include:

  • Executive Coaching: 1:1 coaching tailored to healthcare professionals seeking personal growth, strategic clarity, and greater influence.
  • Leadership Development Programs: Customized workshops and training programs that cultivate confidence, communication, and resilience.
  • Operational Consulting: Strategic insight and problem-solving to help healthcare leaders improve systems, processes, and outcomes.
  • Team Coaching & Retreats: Facilitated group sessions that strengthen team dynamics, reduce burnout, and inspire collective success.

Every session is a safe, structured space to think deeply, act courageously, and grow authentically.

Owner’s Bio

With a B.S. in Sociology and a Master’s degree in Organizational Leadership, Jamie Palma brings extensive experience in non-profit leadership. Her career began with a deep commitment to supporting individuals with disabilities and those navigating mental health challenges.

Over time, she advanced into executive leadership roles—including Deputy Director, Executive Director, and Vice President—before founding Via Coaching, where she now serves as Owner and Operator.

As a Certified Hudson Institute Coach and seasoned executive, Jamie blends leadership coaching with operational precision—offering a unique approach that combines deep expertise in healthcare operations with the transformative power of executive coaching. Her work is structured yet flexible, bold yet supportive—meeting professionals where they are and guiding them toward where they want to be. Through one-on-one coaching, leadership development programs, operational consulting, and team building, she creates safe, strategic spaces for healthcare leaders to think deeply, act courageously, and grow authentically.
